Logistics Glossary

Incoterms: International Commercial Terms that define buyer/seller responsibilities in transactions.
Bill of Lading (BOL): Legal document issued by a carrier acknowledging receipt of cargo for shipment.
FCL/LCL: Full Container Load (exclusive use) vs. Less Than Container Load (shared space).
HAWB: House Air Waybill; freight forwarder's receipt for air cargo.
Customs Bond: Financial guarantee securing payment of duties and taxes.
